The Immune System is a complicated battlefield.

This illustrates the basics.

Mage - Lymphocyte

Skills:

Cast: Spawn

Secretes antibodies, which have a complementary shape to the antigens on the surface of the pathogenic cell. They bind to the antigens and cause them to clump together (agglutination).

Cast: Relief

Antitoxins neutralise toxins released by pathogens. These toxins can disrupt normal cellular processes, cause damage to cells and tissues.

Warrior - phagocyte

Skills:

Cast: Bludgeon

Violently "engulfs and digests (phagocytosis)" nearby pathogens with its blade. Pathogens are located as phagocytes can detect can detect chemicals produced by pathogenic cells.

Cast: Recycle

*Less violently "engulfs and digests" dead or damaged troops preventing the release of cellular debris and other harmful substances that can damage surrounding tissues and lead to inflammation.

Hijacked
